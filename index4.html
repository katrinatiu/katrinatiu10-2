Sweet Treats was established since 2002 and was founded b Ms. Katrina Tiu. It specifically serves sweets such as candies, drink, pastries and desserts. It is usually the comfort place for kids and teenagers. Sweet Treats is consider as "instagrammable" by the customers , with its floral pink wall, marble tables, pink chairs and gold details. It is open for reservations and perfect for celebrations such as birthday party, reception, meetings, even for christening. It's one and only branch is located in the 2nd Floor of venice Grand Canal Mall, Upper McKinley rd in manila.
Come and visit us


Ms. Katrina Tiu, the owner of Sweet Treats established the shop in 2002 for she is a fan of sweets. Katrina believes that life needs a huge dose of sweetness and her cafe will become the source. Katrina wants to maintain the budget friendly prices for she wants to let the people appreciate sweets. She manages the Cafe herself and is supposed to open another branch this year.
